get the smart app

As a rule, smart watches work in conjunction with the phone, connecting to it via Bluetooth. But can a smartwatch work without a phone? Yes they can. There are models based on the Android operating system that do not need to be connected to a mobile gadget. They independently collect data on physical activity, measure heart rate or track sleep, saving summaries to internal memory.

The information is then presented on the display or in the application the next time it is connected to the smartphone.
